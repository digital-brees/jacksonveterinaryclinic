Originally from Cedar Falls, Natalie discovered her passion for animal care while helping care for animals on her grandparents' property in eastern Iowa. "Some of my favorite childhood memories are of caring for animals and learning how much trust and compassion go into their care. Those experiences inspired the way I approach both pets and the people who love them."

"Working in veterinary medicine has always felt incredibly rewarding to me. I love helping pets feel safe and comfortable while supporting their families through every stage of care." Natalie has a special interest in patient care and anesthesia monitoring and enjoys educating pet owners about everyday wellness and preventive care. She brings a calm, compassionate approach to her role at Jackson Veterinary Clinic and is passionate about making every visit a positive experience for both pets and owners.

Outside of the clinic, Natalie enjoys hiking, baking, and spending time with her family. She lives with her husband, their two children, and 5 dogs.
